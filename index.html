Features

Auto Sniping

Effortless sniping made simple. Just provide a contract address, set your preferred settings, and let Dorky Bot do the rest. Our automated system handles tax calculations, maxTx, methodID detection, and first safe block identification. No programming skills needed — just pure sniping power.

Limit Orders

Take control of your trading with smart automation. Set up limit orders to secure profits, protect your investment with stop loss or trailing stop loss, and even automate dip buying at your desired market cap or price. Get the best execution with minimal effort.

Copytrade

Why trade alone? Automatically copy the moves of successful traders while enjoying the same safety features. Let the experts do the heavy lifting while you reap the benefits.

Fast and Secure swaps

Trade with confidence. Our swaps are fortified with MEV-resistant technology, protecting you against sandwich attacks and frontrunning. Combined with ultra-fast execution, Dorky Bot ensures you always get the best buy-in price.

Anti Rug and Reorg protection

Safety comes first. Our advanced anti-rug system offers unmatched protection, boasting an impressive 85% success rate. Dorky Bot even guards you against block reorgs that could leave your trades vulnerable.

Scam and Honeypot Protection

Stay safe from scams before they happen. With Dorky Bot’s built-in simulation system, tokens are thoroughly tested before execution. If a successful sell can’t be simulated, your transaction won’t proceed — all done seamlessly by the bot.
